## Get the Public URL

1. **In Railway, click on your "Aura-FX" service** (the WebSocket one, not MySQL)
2. **Go to "Settings" tab** (you're already there!)
3. **Scroll down to "Networking" section**
4. **Click "Generate Domain"** button (if you haven't already)
5. **Copy the public URL** - it will look like:
   - `https://aura-fx-production.up.railway.app`
   - Or similar format
